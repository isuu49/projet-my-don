Welcome to My Don Restaurant : A Taste of Japan in Every Bowl

At My Don Restaurant, we bring the heart of Japanese comfort food to your table with our expertly crafted ramen and donkatsu. Our chefs blend time-honored techniques with the freshest ingredients to create dishes that are rich in flavor and steeped in tradition.

Each bowl of ramen is a harmony of flavors - from our slow-simmered broths and hand-pulled noodles to the delicate toppings that make every bite unforgettable. Whether you prefer a bold, creamy tonkotsu, a savory shoyu, or a spicy miso ramen, we have a bowl that will warm your soul.

Our donkatsu, a crispy, golden pork cutlet, is another house specialty. Lightly breaded and fried to perfection, it's served with our signature tangy-sweet sauce and a side of fluffy rice and fresh cabbage. It's the perfect balance of texture and taste - crunchy on the outside, juicy on the inside.

Step into My Don and experience the flavors of Japan, crafted with care and served with heart.
